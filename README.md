# DIS Corporation Documentation

Comprehensive technical documentation for the DIS Corporation software ecosystem, covering dealer management systems, equipment manuals, support knowledge bases, and internal training materials.

## Documentation Overview

This knowledge base provides detailed technical documentation across the full DIS Corporation product portfolio. The documentation is organized by product family and functional area to facilitate quick navigation and efficient information retrieval. It is designed to serve both end users seeking operational guidance and support staff requiring in-depth technical references.

## DIS Business Systems

**Keystone** is DIS Corporation's foundational dealer management system, built on the IBM AS/400 platform. This category contains a comprehensive collection of articles covering all aspects of the Keystone system, from initial configuration and user management to advanced reporting, vendor integrations, and end-of-period procedures.

**Quantum** is DIS Corporation's modern, web-based dealer management system. This category provides documentation on Quantum's full feature set, including system setup, user administration, point-of-sale operations, parts management, service workflows, and new feature announcements for each release.

**Quipware & Altura** are specialized applications within the DIS ecosystem. Quipware provides a comprehensive equipment rental and management solution, while Altura offers a modern, web-based platform for service management and mobile field operations.

**System Administration** contains internal-facing documentation for system administrators and DIS support staff, covering server and database management, system-level troubleshooting, internal tools, and operational procedures.

## DIS Business System Manual

The **DIS Business System Manual** is a comprehensive legacy reference document covering all major modules of the DIS Business System, organized by chapters and appendices. It provides detailed information on Parts Management, Point of Sale, Service Management, Accounting, and Vendor Management.

## Kubota Equipment Manuals

This category contains a collection of operator and workshop manuals for various Kubota equipment models, including excavators, wheel loaders, utility vehicles, and attachments.

## Reference

The **Reference** section contains supplementary materials including a structured knowledge base derived from historical Salesforce support cases, providing troubleshooting guidance for common and recurring issues across all DIS products.

## Video Training Knowledge Base

The **Video Training Knowledge Base** is a comprehensive, synthesized reference derived from 43 internal DIS training sessions conducted in 2022. It covers all major modules of the DIS platform — from System Administration and General Ledger through Point of Sale, Parts, Payroll, and CRM — capturing core product knowledge, operational procedures, business rules, and best practices as taught by DIS instructors.
