## DIS System Training Knowledge Base

This section is a comprehensive, synthesized reference to the **DIS (Dealer Information System)** enterprise platform, derived from an in-depth analysis of 43 internal training sessions conducted in 2022. It captures the core product knowledge, system architecture, operational procedures, business rules, and best practices taught across all modules of the DIS platform.

Every concept, procedure, and rule documented here is grounded in the actual training content delivered by DIS instructors. It is designed to serve DIS implementation specialists, support engineers, trainers, and advanced users who need a deep, functional understanding of the platform.
