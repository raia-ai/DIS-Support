### Quantum Refacing
When you access and enter numerous menu options in Release 22v3, you will notice the beginning phases of the Quantum refacing project. This project includes the addition of symbolic icons on the menu option drop-downs that provide a visual reference for the menu option subject as shown below.

The buttons previously positioned on the left side of the screen have been moved to the top of the screen allowing the information to be displayed at a larger more discernible size.
