## Introduction
Sales 360 allows sales representatives to schedule calendar events, view department messages on the Sales 360 dashboard, create and manage sales calls, generate quotes that can instantly be converted to a P.O.S. invoice, and provide/view contact information.

## Provide Sales Representatives Access to Sales 360
Each Sales Representative that you would like to provide access to Sales 360 must be entered into Quantum 360 User Configuration.

1.  Select **Security Maintenance (Security | Security Maintenance)**. The first security maintenance screen opens. Enter the Division and Security Code. Click **Continue**.
