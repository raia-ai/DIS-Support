_Delete a Technician from Altura – Service Logistics_

Introduction 

 This article details how to delete a technician that has been added in Altura. Click here for instructions on adding a technician in Altura. 

  

 To Remove Technician from Altura 

 Deleting a technician involves multiple steps in both Quantum and Altura as shown below. 

   

 In Quantum 

 Select Address Maintenance (Marketing | Address Maintenance) . 

 Provide the technicians Address # and click Enter . 

 

 Unselect the Technician check box and click Enter to save. 

 Select Table Maintenance (P.O.S. | Time Clock | Table Maintenance) . 

 

 Select the Technician Table option, provide the Technician ID and click OK . 

 

 Click the Delete button at the bottom of the screen. 

   

 In Altura 

 Once the technician has been removed from Quantum, you can log into Altura and delete them there using the steps below. 

 

 Select Staff List (Admin Settings | Staff | Staff List) . 

 

 Locate the technician on the screen that opens and click on them. 

 

 Click on the 3 dots next to the word Details as shown above. 

 Select Edit from the Actions menu. 

 Click the Status drop-down arrow on the Edit Staff User screen and select the Disabled option as shown above. 

 Click the Submit button.
