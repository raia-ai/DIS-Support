Introduction 

 This article details how to update staff user information in Altura. 

  

 Update User Information 

 Access Altura. 

 

 Select the Staff card from the dashboard. 

 

 Click on the staff user you wish to edit. The Staff screen displays. 
 Click the ellipsis ( ... )next to the left of Details as shown above. 
 Click the Edit button. The Edit Staff User screen displays. 

 

 Update the information and click Submit . The updated information displays on the Staff screen under the Details pane.
