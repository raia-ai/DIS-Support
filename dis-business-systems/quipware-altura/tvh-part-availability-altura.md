_TVH Part Availability - Altura_

The TVH option under the Manufacturer menu allows you to view available parts, their prices, location, and any available price breaks per quantity purchased. Additionally, orders can be submitted to TVH and price books can be updated and applied to inventory.Â  

 Click the link in the Files box on the right side of the screen to display a PDF providing instruction on how to check the availability of a part and view associated information including part details, warehouses that have the part, and the percentage of discount offered based on the quantity of parts purchased.
