_PMOP Codes - Altura_

When creating operations in PM Management through Altura, a job code must be assigned to the operation as part of the process. Job Codes are created through Quantum where they are identified as PMOP (Planned Maintenance Operation) Codes. Click the link in the Files box on the right side of the screen to view a document detailing how to create these codes.
