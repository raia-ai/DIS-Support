_Merge Bin - QW_

Change Bin/Merge Bin 
 How to move parts from one bin to another? 

  

  

 

  

  

 You can use the merge bin utility in the purchasing module. 

 From QW Purchasing>Receipt, select Change Bin 

 Enter the product ID and branch, click Search. 

  

  

 

  

 Click on both lines, click the Merge button 

 The dialog box for merge displays.  Click the green arrow to point to the location you want to keep.  Click Continue.
