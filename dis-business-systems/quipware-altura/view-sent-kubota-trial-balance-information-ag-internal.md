_View Sent Kubota Trial Balance Information - AG Internal_

Introduction 

 Financial information submitted to Kubota is typically identical to the financial statement in the business system. However, a dealer may need to verify the submitted data. This information can be viewed through Altura. 

 Note : Accessing Altura for this purpose requires the Prism License Altura Tool Communications. 

 Â 

 To View Sent Information 

 Access Altura. 

 

 Select Business System (Tools | Communication | Business System) . 

 Select Sent to Vendor . The Vendor Communications screen displays. 

 

 Click Filters , then click the Message Type drop-down and select Kubota Trial Balance . 

 Click Apply . 

 

 Locate and click View in the Sent Message column. The Sent Message screen opens listing information such as the amount and account number.
