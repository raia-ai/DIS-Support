_PM Management - Altura_

The PM Management option allows your dealership to create and save the routine maintenance performed by your dealership and then have that maintenance automatically or manually scheduled to be performed based on the equipment owned by your customers. Click the link in the Files box on the right side of the screen to download a document detailing PM Management.
