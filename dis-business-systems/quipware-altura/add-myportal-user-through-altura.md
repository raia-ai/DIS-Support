Introduction 

 MyPortal allows users to view various information about their relationship with a dealership such as invoices, statements, and work performed. This article details how to add a MyPortal user through Altura and grant them full or limited access to information. 

  

 Configure MyPortal User 

 Access Altura. 

 

 Select Customer List (Customers | Customer List) . The Customers list displays. 
 Locate and select the customer that you want to configure. Use the search field to locate the customer. The Customer Information displays. 

 

 Select Customer Users from the left-side of the screen, then click the + Customer User button. The Add New Customer screen displays. 

 

 Provide the new customer information and click Submit . Ensure the new user is designated as active on the customer User Account list as shown below. 

 

 Click the user. The User Details screen displays. 

 

 Select the Roles & Permissions option on the left-side of the screen. 
 Select whether you want to give the customer user Full or Limited MyPortal access .
