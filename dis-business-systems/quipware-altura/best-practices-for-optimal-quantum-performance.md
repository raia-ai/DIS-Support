Follow the steps in this article to ensure you can access the information you need in Quantum and stay connected without unnecessary interruptions or logouts. 

 Update your browser to the latest stable version. 

 If you use Chrome as your default browser, copy and paste the following in your address bar at the top of the screen and press Enter : 

 chrome://settings/help 

 If you use Edge as your default browser, copy and paste the following in your address bar at the top of the screen and click Enter . 

 edge://settings/help 

 The browser automatically updates to the latest version. 

 Disable Pop-Up blockers for the Quantum/Altura sites. The site URLs used in this process can be copied and pasted from the address bar when you access the site. 

 

 If you use Chrome , copy and paste the following in your address bar, press Enter , and add BOTH the Quantum and Altura URLS under sites allowed to send pop-ups: 

 Chrome://settings/content/popups 

 If you use Edge , copy and paste the following in your address bar, press Enter , and add Both the Quantum and Altura URLs under sites allowed to send pop-ups: 

 edge://settings/privacy/sitePermissions/allPermissions/popups 

  

 Configure the Quantum site to always be active. 

 

 If you use Chrome as your default browser, copy and paste the following in your address bar and press Enter : 

 chrome://settings/performance 

 Click the Add button, and enter the Quantum URL. 

 If you use Edge as your default browser, copy and paste the following in your address bar and press Enter : 

 edge://settings/system/managePerformance 

 Click the Add site button for the Never put these sites to sleep option and add Quantum URL 

 Ensure you specify a Device Name in your Quantum URL “?DeviceName=”. Click here for instruction on creating Device names. 

 There are multiple Quantum URL’s, add  ?DeviceName=XXXX      to the end similar to below and save it as a bookmark 

 http://111.111.111.111/webclient /?DeviceName=AAAAAAAA_ 
 https://quantumlab.dis.us/webclient/ ?DeviceName=AAAAAAAA_ 

 This helps DIS troubleshoot issues with users and sessions. 

 Remove any Scanning/AV extensions from the browser. 
 Disable Computer Sleep/Hibernation.  If your PC is set to sleep, you will be logged out of Quantum if you are away from your PC for an extended amount of time.
