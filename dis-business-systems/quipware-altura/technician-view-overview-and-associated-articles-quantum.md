_Technician View Overview and Associated Articles - Quantum_

Introduction 

 Technician View allows service managers / users to view what technicians / employees are currently working on based on the information they provided on their most recent Time Clock punch. This article provides an overview of the actions available through Technician View. This article provides an overview of the information in Technician View as well as a brief description of the options that can be taken with links to more in-depth articles detailing those options. 

  

  

 Technician View 

 Select Technician View (P.O.S. | Time Clock | Technician View) . The Technician View screen opens listing information regarding the Time Clock punches of all of the Technicians / Employees who have made them. 

 

 Potential information available includes: 

 Tech – The address assigned to the Technician in Address Maintenance ( Marketing | Address Maintenance). 
 WO# - The number assigned to the Work Order that the technician is working. 
 Customer – The name of the customer and the address number assigned to them in Receivables Maintenance (Accounting | Receivables | Receivables Maintenance) . 
 Status – The Work Status Code  selected by the technician for the Time Clock punch. 
 Make & Model – The Make and Model of the equipment designated in the work order. 
 Max – The Maximum amount/time setup to charge ( Flat Rate ) for the work being done. If a technician has exceeded or is nearing the maximum dollar amount for the job, the entire line will display in red. The lines will display in blue if the technician has reached 50% of the maximum and purple if the technician has reached 75% of the maximum. 
 Actual – The Actual amount of time the technician has spent on the work order. 
 In – When the technician began working on the current job. 
 Elapsed – The amount of time that has elapsed since the technician began work. 
 Tech Name – The name provided to the technician when they were entered in the system. 
 Comment – Comments provided by the technician while entering a time clock punch. 
 Segment – The segment of the work order being worked if the work order was segmented and the technician specified which segment they are working on. 

 To locate a specific technician, enter their address in the Position To Tech field and click the Position To Tech button. 

  

 You may also complete the following options in Technician View: 

 Edit or Delete Now Working On Information - Technicians can update their Now Working On information through Time Clock. Likewise, service managers and other users can update or delete the now working on information for a Time Clock punch through Technician View. This article details how to update or delete the now working on information through Technician View. 

  

 Select Content and Set Layout - Technician View allows users to control what information they see when in Technician View and how it is displayed. This article details how to configure the following in Technician View: 

 Column order 
 Information displayed 
 Column width 

  

 Select Departments to Display - In addition to the using the Change View and Column Configuration options to control the jobs that display in Technician View, the Define List option allows you to control which employees are listed in the table based on the department to which they are assigned. 

  

 Enable Auto Update Mode in Technician View - By default, the information in Technician View remains the same and does not update. However, an option exists that allows you to automatically refresh the information every 15 seconds. This allows you to maintain oversight on the work that is being performed in the shop and ensure that each job is on track throughout the course of the day. 

  

 Important! If the technicians at your dealership use Service Logistics for labor punches, the Now Working On information does not display in Technician View as Technician View does not retrieve information from Service Logistics. Click here for instructions on viewing the Now Working On information in Altura.
