_TVH Configuration - Altura_

The TVH option under the Manufacturer menu allows you to view available parts, their prices, location, and any available price breaks per quantity purchased. Additionally, orders can be submitted to TVH and price books can be updated and applied to inventory. Click the link in the Files box on the right side of the screen to view a PDF providing instruction on how to configure TVH in Altura.
