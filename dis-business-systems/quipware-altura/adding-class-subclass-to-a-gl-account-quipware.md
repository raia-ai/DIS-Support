_Adding Class / Subclass to a GL Account - QW_

From QW Accounting>Setup>Chart of Account, select the account. 

 Select the Class/Subclass button on lower right of screen. 

  

  

 

  

  

 Select the class, click the + to expand the listing of available subclasses, select the one you want, click Add.
