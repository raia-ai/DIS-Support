_Scheduled Accounting Reporting - A Purge job is currently running. Please wait until the purge is complete before trying again. - message - AG Internal_

When trying to get in to Scheduled Account Reporting a message comes up as follows: 

  

 A Purge job is currently running. Please wait until the purge is complete before trying again. 

  

 At a command line, type: 

 WRKF uT.@@NI 

 (Where 'u' = fileset) 

  

 Delete this file.  

 You should now be able to get into Schedule Account Reporting 

  

   

  

 X1473 

 X147-3
