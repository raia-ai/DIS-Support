_POS, PDFs not attaching to email - Ag Internal_

Issue 

 In POS the customer clicks the ‘email’ button.  The PDF opens rather than attaches to the email. 

  

  

 Solution 

 Change the settings in Chrome so that PDF's will 'save' rather than 'open': 

 Click the  ‘settings’ icon in the upper right corner of the browser: 

 

 Click Settings . 

 Select  Privacy and security: 

 

 Click on Site settings 

 

  

 Scroll down to "Contents" and click on ‘Additional content settings’: 

 

 Click ‘PDF Documents’: 

  

 

 

  

 Enable the option to ‘Download PDFs’.
