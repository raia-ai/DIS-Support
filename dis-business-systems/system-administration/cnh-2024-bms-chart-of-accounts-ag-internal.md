_CNH 2024 BMS Chart of Accounts - AG Internal_

Click the PDF link in the Files Box on the right side of this screen to view an overview of the established CNH BMS Chart of Accounts. It is intended to provide some overall guidance to CNH dealer principals/controllers in how to map the dealership chart of accounts into the CNH BMS Chart of Accounts. If the DMS the dealer is working with provides only limited mapping capabilities, please contact the CNH BMS Team to learn about the BMS Automapping tool.
