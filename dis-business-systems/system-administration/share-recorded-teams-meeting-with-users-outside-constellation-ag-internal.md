_Share Recorded Teams Meeting with Users Outside Constellation – AG Internal_

Introduction 

 This article provides two options for sharing a recorded Teams Meeting with users who are not affiliated with Constellation 

  

 Option 1 - From Teams 

 Access the recording in Teams. 
 
 Click the Share option. 

 Ensure the setting is Only the people you specify who have this link….. 

 Add the email address of those you’ll be sharing the link with in the space provided. 

 Click the Send button so the recording is available for users to Download as needed. 

  

 Option 2 - From Vimeo 

 A secure private link can be created for recordings from Vimeo and shared as needed. 

 

 Contact Revathi Narayanan or Chris Hancart for the login credentials to use this option.
