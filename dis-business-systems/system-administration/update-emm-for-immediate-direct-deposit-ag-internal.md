_Update EMM for Immediate Direct Deposit - AG Internal_

Issue 


  

 Solution 

 Open a command line, enter UPDDTA EMM   and click OK . 

 

 Provide the employee address you need to update and click Enter to get to the second screen. 

 Enter Y in both the Direct Deposit Verified and Set Direct Deposit Active fields. 

 

 Press F11 to update, then save and exit by clicking Enter again.
