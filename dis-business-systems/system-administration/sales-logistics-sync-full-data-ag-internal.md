_Sales Logistics - Sync Full Data - AG Internal_

Sales Logistics – Sync Full Data 

   

 Introduction 

 If a unit isn’t showing in Sales Logistics a data sync may resolve it. 

   

 Sync Full Data 

 Tap the ‘Hamburger’ menu icon then tap About. 

 

 This will open the About information screen. 

 Tap the circle to sync data. 

 

 You’ll see a Syncing update as the job is processing.
