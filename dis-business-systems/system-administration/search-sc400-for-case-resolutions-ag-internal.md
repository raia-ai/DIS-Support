Access SC400 : 

 

 Enter IP address 172.16.16.741 and click Mocha to access the SC400. 

 

 Type SUPPORT on the command line and press Enter . 

 

 Type 1 in the Selection field and press Enter . 

 

 Type 4  in the Selection field and click Enter to select Queue status. 

 

 Type 1 in the Opt column on the ENHANC line and press Enter . 

 

 Press F2 . 

 

 Press F10 . 

 Place a 5 to look at a call then press F10 for the comments.
