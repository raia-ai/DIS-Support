_Enable/Disable Dropship for Internal Invoices OPT - AG Internal_

Issue 

 The customer wants to allow or prevent dropship from being used for internal work orders/invoices. 

  

 Solution 

 Enter  X000081 POSDROPI,1  on a command line and click OK to enable/disable OPT.
