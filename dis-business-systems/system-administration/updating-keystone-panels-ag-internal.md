_Updating Keystone Panels - AG Internal_

Here are instructions to refresh the Keystone sessions, to grab the current Keystone update. Especially after a business system upgrade. 

 If users are having issues with an incomplete Keystone compile date on the PC; have the user try this on their PC with the issue. 

 This could be small windows, font issues, "reverting to Green screen" and things of the like. 

  

 Please do the following to force Keystone to pick up the panels again the PC with the problem. 

  

 Close down all Keystone sessions 

 Navigate to C:\Program Files (x86)\DIS\Keystone\Keystone folder 

 Delete the Keystone.CSH file 

  

 

  

 When you go to restart Keystone – BE SURE TO RIGHT CLICK on the Keystone icon and run in ADMINISTRATOR MODE.
