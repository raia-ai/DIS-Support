_PMR - ITS file codes - AG Internal_

Occasionally we run in to issues with the Parts Management Report (PMR) and data needs to be corrected manually. 
  
 Below is a guide to the coding used for the ITSPMR field in the ITS file.  
  
  
 SA – Sales 
             Counter                                 SACT 
             Service                                  SASV 
             Internal                                  SAIN 
             Warranty                                SAWA 
   
 RT – Returns                                    
             Counter                                 RTCT 
             Service                                  RTSV 
             Internal                                  RTIN 
   
 RC – Receipts                                              
             Priority Order                         RCPR 
             Stock Order                           RCST 
             Other                                     RCOT 
   
 IA – Inventory Adjustments            
             Returns to Supplier               IARS 
             Transfers In                           IATI 
             Transfers Out                        IATO 
             Reprice Parts                        IAPU   
             Price Updates                       IAPU 
             Part Adjustments                   IAPT 
             Phase Outs                           IAPO 
             Parts Data Entry                   IADE 
             Special                                  IASP 
   
 LS – Lost Sales 
             Lost Sales                             LSLS 
   
 PI – Performance Indicators          
             Pieces first time                    PIPF 
             Pieces After Order                PIPO 
   
 AC – Average Cost Audit Record     ACCH
