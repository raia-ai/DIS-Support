_Record Length of Parts Warranty (Maintain Parts Warranty) - Quantum_

Introduction 

 This option allows you to keep a record of the number of days a specific part from a vendor is under warranty. You can change the length of time or delete the information once it has been added. 

  

 To Record Length of Parts Warranty 

 Select  Maintain Parts Warranty (Service | Service Utilities | Maintain Parts Warranty) . The Maintain Parts Warranty screen opens in Add mode. If it opens in Change mode, click the Add button. 

    

 Click in the Vendor column and type the code for the vendor of the part whose warranty length you are recording. 

 Type the part number and warranty length in the respective columns. 

 Click  Enter . The warranty information is saved and the information in the table is cleared. 

 Click  Exit  to return to the main menu. 

  

 To Change the Part Warranty Length 

 The Vendor and Part # cannot be changed once they have been entered. If either of those need to be changed, you will need to delete the entry and re-enter it correctly. 

 Select  Maintain Parts Warranty (Service | Service Utilities | Maintain Parts Warranty) . The Maintain Parts Warranty screen opens in Change mode. 

 

 Click in the Days Warranty column and enter the correct length of days the part is under warranty. Click Enter to save. 

  

 To Delete a Part Warranty Entry 

 Select  Maintain Parts Warranty (Service | Service Utilities | Maintain Parts Warranty) . The Maintain Parts Warranty screen opens in Change mode. 

 Click on the Part Warranty line that you would like to remove and click the Delete button. The Part Warranty information is removed from the table.
