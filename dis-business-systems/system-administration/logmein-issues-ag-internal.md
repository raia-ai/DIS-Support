_LogMeIn Issues - AG Internal_

Issue 

 LogMeIn won’t connect regardless of browser. 

  

 Solution 

 Select the Link tab, click the Copy Link to Clipboard button and send the link to the customer in an Email. 

   

 Alternatively, you can select the Email tab, enter the customer Email address and send them the link, but this method has failed previously.
