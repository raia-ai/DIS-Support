_Resend Equipment Data for Sales Logistics - Ag Internal_

If the Units / Equipment data get out of sync, you may need to resend the data to the middle tier.  

 To do this, you must be signed on as either SUPPORT or QSECOFR, at a command line type:  

 EQ_Load   F4 

 Enter fileset and enter. 

 Put in a date at is old enough to pull in the units that are missing information. 

 Change download pictures to Y. 

 Enter. 

  

 Wait 15 min or so. 

 Then in Sales Logistics on a phone or tablet, click about and resync. 

 Check units/units with an issue. 

  

 To get to the About option in the App, Swipe from left to right while in the App. 

 Click the down arrow to the right of the word about.  

 Click on Sync full data at the bottom.
