## INTRODUCTION

Part supersession is the replacement of a part with an interchangeable part. When a part is superseded to another part, a supersession chain is formed. When a supersession chain consists of multiple parts, there is a potential for backward supersession and forward supersession.

Supersession information can come from price books or be entered manually through Parts Posting & Maintenance. You can view backward and forward supersession information for a part through Parts Inquiry or while on the Document Detail screen in Invoicing.

This document covers how to manually enter part supersession information as well as how to set the system to search for backwards supersession information. It also instructs you how to view supersession information in Invoicing when it is found.
