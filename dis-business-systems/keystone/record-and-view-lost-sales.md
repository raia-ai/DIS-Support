## Introduction
A customer can decide not to purchase a part for a variety of reasons such as price or immediate availability. When a customer doesn't make a purchase, you can record it in your sales history. Demand associated with lost sales is considered when calculating a Suggested Stock Order and recording lost sales will generate a more accurate Suggested Stock Order. Additionally, if lost sales are recorded, a report of lost sales by vendor or a particular customer can be created.

## Record Lost Sales
You can record lost sales using the Parts Posting and Maintenance or Invoicing menu options.
