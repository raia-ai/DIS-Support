## Introduction
Unit Makes and Models are often incorrectly entered under different names. As a result, Makes and Models in your inventory or belonging to customers can be overlooked when working with Units. It is best practice to routinely search for and update Makes and Models that have been entered incorrectly. This document covers how to merge unit Makes and Models under the correct name.
