## Introduction
This article provides instruction on the Market Basket Data Extraction for Agco.

## Instructions

1.  Select **Keystone Data Mine** from the Keystone main menu (**System | Keystone Data Mine**). The **File New** screen opens. In the screen, select the **Point of Sale** category and the **Point of Sale Document Detail Information** subcategory.
